Low magnesium means weaker brakes and a louder accelerator — at the same time.

The brake
GABA — your brain's slow-down signal.
Magnesium helps activate it.
The accelerator
NMDA — the neuron's go-switch.
Magnesium keeps it regulated.
That's why low-mag symptoms cluster around jumpiness — poor sleep, anxiety, twitches, migraines.
